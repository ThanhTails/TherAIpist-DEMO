How to use-----------------------------------------------------------------


------First, enter a dolphin chatting page------

Enter the dolphin chatting page by clicking "Dolphins" in the menu bar or "Chat now" button.



------And, get ready to chat------

You are in the dolphin chatting page! Now, let's chat with him/her. To ask, you must use English language, or else he/she will ask you to asking again.

It will answer as simple as possible for you to know about things you asked for.

------How to stop chat?------

Just CLOSE or GO BACK from the page. But if you want, just say "Goodbye" before CLOSE the page
